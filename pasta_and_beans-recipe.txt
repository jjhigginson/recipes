Ingredients:
  - 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  - 4-5 medium plum tomatoes, chopped
  - 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  - Pinch of crushed red pepper
  - 1 15-ounce can of cannellini beans
  - 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  - freshly ground pepper to taate
  - 8 ounces oreccheitte, radiatore or other short pasta
  - 1 pound brocalli rabe, stem ends trimmed, rinsed and cut into 1-1/2 inch pieces
  - 1/4 cup freshly grates parmesan cheese
